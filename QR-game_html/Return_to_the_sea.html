Many groups of vertebrate animals have evolved from terrestrial forms to become partially or fully aquatic. Animals like whales and dolphins are descended from land dwelling mammals, which is why they lack gills and must return to the surface for air. While many people think mosasaurs are dinosaurs, they are actually giant, aquatic lizards!
