While evolution has trends driven by natural selection, it is the result of random mutations. An individual animal may have beneficial or harmful mutations, which will affect its chances of survival and reproduction. Animals with adaptive traits pass on their genes and the population as a whole will evolve to better survive its environment... or perish trying.
